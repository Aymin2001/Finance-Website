Freedom Finance Technologies

Freedom Finance Technologies Ltd. is a multinational team that develops tech solutions and infrastructure for all clients and employees of Freedom Holding Corp.

Freedom Holding Corp.

Freedom Holding Corp is an international investment holding company catering to over 230,000 clients in the US, Europe, and Central Asia under the Freedom Finance brand.

Freedom Holding stock has been traded on the Nasdaq stock exchange since October 2019 under the ticker FRHC. As of June 2023, the holding's market cap exceeds $5B.

Regulation

Freedom Holding is regulated by the US's Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). The EU division is licensed by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission (CySEC).

Tradernet

Tradernet supplies brokerage services through a web trading platform and mobile apps, a CRM system, back-end tools, exchange gateways, and APIs for integration with websites, payment systems and automated KYC/AML systems.

Tradernet users can trade 40,000 financial instruments across various asset classes: stocks, ETFs, futures, options, and bonds.

Mobile Apps

Tradernet and Freedom24 apps for Android, iOS, and Huawei enable opening an investment account with Freedom Finance, buying and selling stocks, and submitting applications to trade IPOs.
